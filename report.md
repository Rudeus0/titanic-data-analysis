# Titanic Survival Insights

## 1. Gender Impact
Women had a significantly higher survival rate (74%) compared to men (18%).

## 2. Class Advantage
First-class passengers had a survival rate of 63%, much higher than third-class passengers (24%).

## 3. Family Size Effect
Passengers traveling in small families had the highest survival probability.

## 4. Age Distribution
Most passengers were between 20 and 40 years old.
